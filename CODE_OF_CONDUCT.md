# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `privacy-proxy` to be a practical, respectful and safe place to work on log privacy tooling. Contributors and maintainers are expected to participate with care, patience and good intent.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep discussions focused on the work, evidence and tradeoffs.
- Use synthetic examples when discussing sensitive data issues.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Help make the project safer and easier to use.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing real secrets, customer logs, tokens, credentials or personal data in issues, PRs or discussions.
- Repeatedly derailing technical conversations.
- Any conduct that would make reasonable contributors feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainer privately. Do not include real secrets or sensitive logs in reports.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users or take other action needed to keep the project healthy.
